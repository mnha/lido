# appellationValue \[Beginning of existence\]

### Definition

#### LIDO

Appellations, e.g. titles, identifying phrases, or names given to an item, but also name of a person or corporation, also place name etc.

#### MuseumPlus

Réf. géo. \[type=lieu de création\]

### Attributes

**@xml:lang**

`de` \| `en`
